About Research

I am an economist with an expertise in impact evaluation of public policies and interventions advancing women’s economic empowerment.

Since 2023 I have been working at the World Bank, Africa Gender Innovation Lab. I explore how legal and policy reforms, coupled with innovative field interventions, can strengthen women’s human capital, unlock productivity, and drive inclusive prosperity.

I received my PhD in economics from the University of Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne (2023) and my Master in Law from the Ecole Normale Supérieure (2017).
